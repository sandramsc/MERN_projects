Biography

While there’s no sure route to carving out a significant presence in the music industry, there’s always something to be said for remaining true to one’s self. Which is to say that his art, not unlike his life, has been as much about inspiring the streets, and about justice, freedom, and interrogating real-world issues, as it is about his personal journey of faith.
